Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app → Go to Settings → Tap "Restore Purchases"

How do I use scientific notation?

Tap the ×10ⁿ button and enter your exponent, then press = to apply.

How do I enter fractions?

Enter the numerator, tap /, then enter the denominator.

How do I change the sign (positive/negative)?

Long press the 0 button to toggle between positive and negative.

Is the app available offline?

Yes! All conversions work without internet connection.
